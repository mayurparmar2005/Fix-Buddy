Who We Are

Who We Are

Fix Buddy is Ahmedabad's most trusted on-demand home service marketplace. We were born from a simple struggle: finding a reliable plumber, cleaner, or beautician shouldn't be harder than ordering a pizza.

We bridge the gap between busy households in areas like Satellite, Vastrapur, and Iskon, and skilled local professionals who take pride in their work. Whether you need a deep home clean, a quick AC repair, or a relaxing salon session at home, Fix Buddy is your one-stop solution.

Our Mission

Our mission is twofold:

  • To Simplify Living: We deliver safety, quality, and convenience to your doorstep, giving you back your weekends.
  • To Empower Professionals: We provide a dignified platform for skilled workers (our "Buddies") to earn a steady income, manage their schedules, and grow their careers without middlemen exploitation.
